Basic Travel and Arrival Primer

Travel and Arrival

BWI, IAD and DCA are the closest airports to UMBC

EKTA provides pickups from the BWI Airport Only

If you are arriving at IAD / DCA, you can get a cab or use Supershutle

According to Customs rule, an individual is allowed to carry not more than $ 10000 with him/her while travelling to USA. Out of the allowed $ 10000, the individual may carry not more than $ 3000 as cash and the rest $ 7000 in the form of TCs or Forex Card.

Housing Information

Off campus housing is by far the most popular choice at UMBC

Most of us live around Westland Garden Apartments. EKTA helps you find and lease houses, please keep an eye out for the housing forms on our Facebook Group


Cost Estimates : Off Campus Housing

~1000$ (Rent)
~120$ (Utilities)

EKTA also provides temporary housing when you arrive in Baltimore for a limited time.

Information about temporary housing will also be available on our facebook group.

We will update you with details and contact information of your host well before you leave for UMBC.
