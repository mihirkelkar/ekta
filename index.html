According to Customs rule, an individual is allowed to carry not more than $ 10000 with him/her while travelling to USA. Out of the allowed $ 10000, the individual may carry not more than $ 3000 as cash and the rest $ 7000 in the form of TCs or Forex Card.

There are several opportunities with active research being pursued in many STEM Fields. Getting a TA / RA in Computer Science should not be a very difficult ask. Assistantship opportunities in IS are unlikely. Jobs like graders are readlily available. The best way to go about RA opportunities is to present yourself well, be clear about your research interests and have a casual discussion with the Professor. The Professors do not have enough time to read your long mails. A concise mail with your research interests and an appointment to have a discussion should suffice.

A lot of students work Campus Jobs. Several jobs are available in the library, food courts, reception desks, residential life and several other places. Be sure to have your resume approved from Career Services before you apply for on campus jobs. Students on an F1 Visa are allowed to work 20 hours a week on campus.References from seniors can improve your chances of securing the job.

Most recent information about UMBC's immunization requirements can be found on the University Health Services Page


  • For MMR, Hepatitis B, Chicken Pox etc. if you have already taken the required vaccination, you will need to submit a copy of the original records or a blood test reporting you are immunized against these specific conditions.

  • In case you are not sure whether you have completed the dosage, the best course of action would be to just take the vaccinations now.

  • Please carry a letter from your doctor certifying that you have recieved vaccinations in India.

  • Bank of America

    Opening an account here is advisable because it is spread all over US . You will find lot of Bank of America ATM's in every state. Also it is very easy to transfer money from you account to your friend's account through online banking. You need your Passport, I20, UMBC Id and your University Admit letter to open an account here. You will get a temporary card when you open an account and the permanent card will be shipped to your address within a week.

  • PNC Bank

    This bank is located on campus. There are three PNC bank ATMs on campus. Transfering money from PNC to other banks takes time. However, the bank usually offers a 75$ credit on your first direct deposit. A lot of people use this bank for their University Payrolls.
